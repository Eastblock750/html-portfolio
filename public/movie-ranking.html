The Best Movies According to Marcel

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Lone Survivor

Lone Survivor" is a war film based on a true story about a team of Navy SEALs whose mission in Afghanistan goes wrong, leaving only one man struggling to survive.

Law Abiding Citizen

Law Abiding Citizen is a thriller about a man who takes justice into his own hands after a flawed legal system fails to punish his family's killers.

The White House Down

The White House Down is an action movie about a secret service agent who must protect the President and stop a terrorist attack on the White House.
